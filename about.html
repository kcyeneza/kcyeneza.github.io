ABOUT ME

a few things worth knowing.

I was born in Kuwait City, and moved to Pagosa Springs, Colorado when I turned 5. It definitely took a lot of adjusting relocating to such a small town, however, it definitely broadened my experience of life. In high school, I was very involved with band, music performance, and art, so naturally I gravitated towards a major that allowed me to be curious about creativity and it's correspondance to technology. I've come a long way since high school, and fitness has become a big part of my life! I love staying active, and going to the gym has truly felt like one of the places where I can be most like myself.


As I continue to grow, there are still many things that have stuck with me since day one. Something that I am so appreciative to have, is a close relationship with my family. I have three sisters, all whom I'm extremely close with, and people that I consider my best friends. We bond over a lot of the same hobbies, and it was my younger sister, Beatrice, that got me hooked on lifting! I also have so much adoration for my dad, Francis Yeneza. He has taught me about all the important qualities to maintaining a balanced life, and in most ways I attribute my best qualities to him. My family has always been so supportive of my hobbies, which has allowed me to pursue the things that I want to.


I still also maintain a high level of interest towards music. I love making music and majoring in Creative Tech and Design has only encouraged my appreciation towards music. With technology, music has really trancended and it's super cool to be a part of life where so many genres exist!
